Trial ID: NCT002
Title: Semaglutide vs Placebo in Obese Type 2 Diabetes Patients
Condition: Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus with Obesity
Phase: III
Sponsor: AIIMS Diabetes Research Centre
Duration: 68 weeks
Location: Delhi, India

Inclusion Criteria:
- Age between 18 and 70 years
- Type 2 Diabetes diagnosis confirmed by physician
- HbA1c between 8.0% and 12.0%
- BMI >= 30 kg/m2
- eGFR >= 30 mL/min/1.73m2
- ECOG performance status 0, 1, or 2
- On stable antidiabetic therapy for at least 3 months

Exclusion Criteria:
- Personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma
- Multiple Endocrine Neoplasia syndrome type 2
- Prior use of GLP-1 receptor agonists within 6 months
- Severe gastrointestinal disease
- Recent cardiovascular event within 3 months
- Current insulin pump therapy
