Trial ID: NCT003
Title: Metformin Plus Lifestyle Intervention in Pre-diabetes
Condition: Pre-diabetes / Impaired Glucose Tolerance
Phase: II
Sponsor: Tata Memorial Hospital
Duration: 24 weeks
Location: Mumbai, India

Inclusion Criteria:
- Age between 25 and 65 years
- Fasting glucose between 100 and 125 mg/dL
- HbA1c between 5.7% and 6.4%
- eGFR >= 60 mL/min/1.73m2
- ECOG performance status 0
- No current antidiabetic medication
- Willing to participate in lifestyle intervention program

Exclusion Criteria:
- Established Type 2 Diabetes diagnosis
- Current use of any antidiabetic medication
- Severe renal impairment (eGFR < 60)
- Active liver disease
- Recent major surgery within 6 months
- Uncontrolled hypertension (BP > 180/110)
