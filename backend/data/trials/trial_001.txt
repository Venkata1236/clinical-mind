Trial ID: NCT001
Title: Empagliflozin in Type 2 Diabetes with Cardiovascular Risk
Condition: Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus
Phase: III
Sponsor: Apollo Hospitals Research Division
Duration: 52 weeks
Location: Hyderabad, India

Inclusion Criteria:
- Age between 30 and 75 years
- Diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus for at least 1 year
- HbA1c between 7.5% and 10.5%
- eGFR >= 45 mL/min/1.73m2
- ECOG performance status 0 or 1
- Currently on Metformin monotherapy or dual oral therapy

Exclusion Criteria:
- Type 1 Diabetes or LADA
- Prior insulin therapy within last 3 months
- eGFR < 45 mL/min/1.73m2
- History of diabetic ketoacidosis
- Severe hepatic impairment
- Current use of SGLT2 inhibitors
- Pregnancy or breastfeeding
